# Paying for an Order

## What it does
Pay for an order you've placed, and check the payment status of any of your orders. Once
you've paid, your order moves forward so the sellers can start getting it ready.

## Who can use it
You'll need to be signed in, and you can only pay for and view payments on your own orders.

## How it works
When you're ready, choose how you'd like to pay and submit your payment. Once it goes
through, your order is marked as paid and moves along so each shop can begin preparing your
items. You can pay for an order once — if it's already been paid, we won't charge it again.

You can choose from a few payment methods: card, cash on delivery, PayPal, or bank
transfer. A payment can be pending, paid, failed, or refunded.

## Good to know
You can only pay for and view your own orders. If you ever need a refund, that's handled by
the Shopit team — when a refund is issued, the related parts of the order are cancelled for
you.
