# Becoming a Seller

## What it does
Love shopping on Shopit and want to start selling too? Anyone can open their own shop.
Becoming a seller sets you up with a seller account and your very first store, so you can
start listing products and reach shoppers across Shopit.

## How to become a seller
You become a seller by signing up as one. It's a quick sign-up where, along with your usual
details, you also pick a **name for your store** (you can add a short description too, if
you'd like). As soon as you sign up, your seller account and your first store are created
together — no extra steps to set the store up separately.

Becoming a seller is its own sign-up rather than a switch you flip on your shopper account,
so you'll go through the seller sign-up to get started.

## What happens next
Your new store doesn't go live the very moment you create it. It starts out waiting for a
quick review by the Shopit team, and once it's approved it becomes visible to shoppers and
ready to take orders. In the meantime you can already get a head start — you're free to add
and set up your products while your store is under review, and they'll appear to shoppers as
soon as your store is approved.

## Good to know
- Every store gets a friendly web address of its own so shoppers can find and browse it.
- Once you're a seller, you'll have tools to manage your store, list and update products,
  and keep an eye on your orders.
- If a store ever isn't approved, or is paused later on, it simply won't be visible to
  shoppers during that time.
