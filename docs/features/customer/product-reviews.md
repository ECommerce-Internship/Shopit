# Product Reviews and Ratings

## What it does
Rate a product from 1 to 5 stars and, if you like, leave a written comment. Anyone can read
a product's reviews and see its average rating.

## Who can use it
Reading reviews is open to everyone — no account needed. To write, edit, or delete a review
you'll need to be signed in, and you can only review something you've actually received.

## How it works
Open a product's reviews to see its average rating, how many reviews it has, and the
reviews themselves — each one shows who wrote it, their star rating, their comment, and the
date. To leave your own, give the product a rating from 1 to 5 and, if you'd like, add a
comment of up to 1000 characters.

A couple of simple rules keep reviews genuine:

- You can only review something you've received.
- You can review a given product once.

If you change your mind, you can edit your review within 48 hours of posting it, and it has
to be your own review. You can delete your own review whenever you like.

## Good to know
The star rating and review count you see on a product in the catalog come straight from
reviews like yours.
